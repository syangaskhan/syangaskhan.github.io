Stephanie Y. Yang is a scientist who dabbles in [writing](writing.md) and [puzzle design](https://stephanieyangwrites.wordpress.com/). She is originally from Maryland and now braves cold Boston winters. Her favorite hobby is trying out new hobbies. Some of her hobbies are playing soccer, rock climbing, and [arranging music](https://musescore.com/user/32124443).

Her debut novella, [Strak's Bad Day](https://www.kobo.com/ww/en/ebook/strak-s-bad-day?srsltid=AfmBOoqlm7BJqwtfLNQWZrMuUML9IIiZlJ9M0DiRimcv316QhXGk-g3p), was recently published as an [audiobook](https://open.spotify.com/show/0B0vMrunhawfemrHOdaIht?si=17dd8cf4316743e8) by the [Spotify Audiobook Selects program](https://newsroom.spotify.com/2025-10-16/spotify-audiobook-selects-first-indie-releases/).
