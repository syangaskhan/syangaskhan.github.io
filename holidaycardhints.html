Puzzle 1: Holiday budgeting

  1. A cryptarithm is a puzzle where each letter maps to a digit.
  2. Since we know that F is zero, and in the tens digit of AGAIN+GANG = PAINT, we see that I + N = N. This can only happen if I is a certain number
  3. I is equal to 9.
  4. In the 10,000 digit place of AGAIN + GANG = PAINT, we see that A + nothing = P. This must mean something was carried over from the previous digit place, and A + 1 = P.
  5. Since we know I = 9, and HI * HI = YAK, we can figure out what the ones digit (K) is (9 * 9 = ?).

  6. F = 0
    K = 1
    H = 2
    T = 3
    A = 4

  7. P = 5
    N = 6
    G = 7
    Y = 8
    I = 9

Puzzle 2: White Elephant

  1. Each clue corresponds to a color and a creature.
  2. For example, "the odd one out" refers to a "black sheep".
  3. Once you have all the color-animal pairs, use the number in parentheses and take that letter. For example, taking the 7th letter of black sheep (7), gives you an "H".
